School has been nurturing learning opportunities for students from all walks of life since early 2017. 
Launching with a flagship class of just six students, today Footsteps stands in a professionally designed, purpose-built facility, with experienced , professional educators who have impacted hundreds of young students’ lives.
Farhana Hussain is the heart and soul behind the very foundation of Footsteps, with over 25 years of experience in teaching preschool and primary students,
Farhana established Footsteps from a humble environment, cultivating and nurturing the school to great provenance and repute. Each member of her exceptional team is hand-picked to contribute their unique skill sets in making the school an unparalleled institute. 
Maha Jamal Qazi serves as the Operations Director of Footsteps School. Maha completed her Law degree from the University of Buckingham in England, where she began her career as a lawyer.
After relocating to Pakistan, Maha joined forces with Ms. Farhana to launch Footsteps and currently leads the administration, marketing and outreach departments. Additionally, the admission offices, admin teams, school events, creative modules and parent workshops at Footsteps are imagined, managed, and executed under Maha’s direction.
